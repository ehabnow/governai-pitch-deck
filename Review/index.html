01 - Problem

Compliance teams are buried in documents, deadlines, and disconnected evidence.

Saudi regulated companies are facing more rules, more digital audits, more privacy expectations, and more board-level governance pressure. The operating model is still mostly manual.

The pain Manual readiness work is slow, expensive, and hard to defend. Gaps surface late during audits, bank diligence, board reviews, tenders, or regulator requests.
01 Regulations expand

Financial, privacy, cyber, tax, and governance frameworks keep changing.

02 Evidence is scattered

Policies, screenshots, approvals, contracts, and audit files live everywhere.

03 Consultants fill the gap

Manual readiness work becomes expensive, slow, and hard to repeat.

04 Leaders see risk late

Gaps surface during audits, bank due diligence, board reviews, or inspections.
