03 - Old workflow

The current workflow is a cost trap.

Today

  1. Regulation arrives as PDF, circular, or control list.
  2. Compliance team interprets it manually.
  3. Evidence requests go out by email and spreadsheet.
  4. Consultants review gaps.
  5. Management receives a late static report.

With GovernAI

  1. Regulations become structured obligations.
  2. Evidence is mapped to each obligation.
  3. Weak or missing proof is surfaced early.
  4. Human reviewers approve final findings.
  5. Executives get a bilingual readiness pack.
